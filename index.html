I am a doctoral student at USC Rossier researching K-12 Education Policy. My primary research area is school finance. Other areas of interest include school choice and LGBT education policy. I aim to leverage the methodological tools (e.g. causal inference approaches, multilevel modeling) which are more common in the study of finance to my work in other areas. My goals are to produce policy-relevant, timely work that has the potential to improve student outcomes and experiences.

If you want to learn more about my work feel free to check out my CV or Google Scholar. This website is a work in progress so if you run into any broken links feel free to email me. pardonic@usc.edu
